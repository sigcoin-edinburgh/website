SigCoin

SigCoin is a Special Interest Group of CompSoc, focusing on cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology. We aim to connect people with these interests and explore this technology and its potential applications from societal and financial perspectives.

Events

We host talks featuring academics and professionals with expertise in the field and workshops on technical topics and other aspects of cryptocurrencies.

You can keep up with our events on Facebook.
